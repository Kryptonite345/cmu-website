Welcome to the City of Malabon University - Admission for Academic Year 2025-2026

Dear Students,

Subject:

On behalf of the entire City of Malabon University (CMU) community, I am delighted to extend a warm and heartfelt welcome to you as a newly admitted student for the academic year 2025-2026. Congratulations on reaching this exciting milestone in your academic journey! We are thrilled that you have chosen CMU as the institution where you will pursue your dreams, develop your skills, and grow into the professional and compassionate individual you aspire to be.

As an institution that prides itself on fostering academic excellence, integrity, and social responsibility, we are committed to providing a nurturing environment where you will be empowered to reach your fullest potential. Whether you are continuing your studies from high school, or returning to education after a break, we are here to support you every step of the way.

Our dynamic faculty, innovative teaching methods, and state-of-the-art facilities are all designed to create an immersive learning experience that will prepare you for the challenges of tomorrow. In addition to our rigorous academic programs, CMU also offers a variety of extracurricular activities, student organizations, and community service initiatives, all of which are integral to your holistic development.

Contact Us:

Should you have any queries or require clarification, our dedicated team is here to assist you. Reach out to us via email at u.admissions@cityofmalabonuniversity.edu.ph or visit our Admission and Scholarship Unit, located at Room 103 Innovation Bldg., City of Malabon University. Join us in the Pursuit of Excellence! We look forward to welcoming you to our vibrant campus and wish all applicants the best of luck in their academic endeavors.
